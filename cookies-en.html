Cookie Policy

Last updated: May 21, 2026

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, smartphone) when you visit a website. They allow the site to remember your actions and preferences (such as language, display settings, etc.) for a certain period.

2. How we use cookies

Alina Coaching uses cookies to improve your browsing experience and provide personalized features. We use the following types of cookies:

2.1 Essential cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic features such as navigation and access to secure areas of the site.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
preferredLanguage Stores your language preference 1 year
session_id Maintains your session Session

2.2 Functional cookies

These cookies allow the website to remember your choices (such as your username, language, or region) and provide enhanced, more personalized features.

2.3 Analytics cookies

We use analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with our website. This helps us improve the user experience. These cookies collect information anonymously.

3. Third-party cookies

Some cookies may be placed by third-party services that appear on our pages. We have no control over these cookies. We recommend that you check the privacy policies of these third parties for more information.

4. Managing cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. You can delete all cookies already on your computer and configure most browsers to prevent them from being placed. However, if you do this, you may have to manually adjust some preferences each time you visit a site, and some services and functionalities may not work.

How to manage cookies in your browser:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies and website data
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions > Cookies and site data

5. Consent

By continuing to browse our website, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy. You can withdraw your consent at any time by deleting cookies from your browser.

6. Updates to this policy

We may update this cookie policy from time to time. We encourage you to review this page regularly to stay informed of any changes.
