Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 21, 2026

1. Introduction

Alina Bevz, self-employed operating under the name "Alina Coaching" in Bratislava, Slovakia, is committed to protecting the confidentiality and security of your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use and protect your personal information in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

2. Data Controller

Name: Alina Bevz
Activity: Fitness and wellness coach
Location: Bratislava, Slovakia
Email: alina@coaching-bratislava.com

3. Data Collected

We only collect data that you voluntarily provide to us via our contact form:

  • Name: To identify you and personalize our response
  • Email address: To respond to you
  • Phone number (optional): To contact you if necessary
  • Message: To understand your request

4. Legal Basis for Processing

The processing of your personal data is based on:

  • Your consent: By submitting the contact form, you consent to the processing of your data
  • Contract performance: To respond to your coaching service request

5. Use of Data

Your personal data is used only to:

  • Respond to your inquiries
  • Provide you with information about our coaching services
  • Schedule coaching sessions if you wish

We do not sell, rent or share your data with third parties.

6. Retention Period

Your data is retained:

  • Contact requests: Maximum 1 year after your last contact
  • Active clients: During our business relationship + 3 years (legal obligations)

After this period, your data is automatically deleted.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:

  • Secure hosting on Netlify (ISO 27001 certified)
  • Encrypted HTTPS connection
  • Restricted data access (Alina Bevz only)
  • Regular backups

8. Your Rights (GDPR)

In accordance with GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access: Obtain a copy of your personal data
  • Right to rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Right to restriction: Limit the processing of your data
  • Right to portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Right to object: Object to the processing of your data
  • Right to withdraw consent: At any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at: alina@coaching-bratislava.com

9. Cookies

Our website does not use any tracking or analytics cookies. We only use cookies strictly necessary for the website to function (for example, to remember your language choice).

10. Data Transfer Outside EU

Your data is hosted on Netlify (United States), which is GDPR compliant and provides appropriate safeguards for the protection of your data.

11. Changes to this Policy

We reserve the right to modify this privacy policy. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new update date.

12. Complaints

If you believe your rights are not being respected, you can file a complaint with:

  • Slovakia: Úrad na ochranu osobných údajov (ÚOOÚ)
  • France: Commission Nationale de l'Informatique et des Libertés (CNIL)
